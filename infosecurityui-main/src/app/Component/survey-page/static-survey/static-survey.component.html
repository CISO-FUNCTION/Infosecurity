Overall Data Security and Data Privacy

Purpose

The purpose of this document is to convey the key DOs and DONTs w.r.t information security, privacy, and the potential impact on {{brandConfig.companyShortName}} when these are not followed. In addition, we have provided consequences for non-adherence and the process for requesting exceptions.

Definitions

Terms used Definition
Credentials The user ID and / or password used to gain access to any {{brandConfig.companyShortName}}, client, or any third party application or system.
Confidential information/ Official Data ({{brandConfig.companyShortName}} or Client) Business-related information or documents that the organization does not intend to make public or share beyond the {{brandConfig.companyShortName}} team.
Information Security Event/Security Breach A cyber event or security breach is any incident that results in unauthorized access to computer data, applications, networks or devices, that could potentially lead to a compromise of the confidentiality, integrity or availability of any information or system.
Privacy Incident Shall be defined as any activity performed in contravention of applicable privacy and data protection laws and regulations of the country.
Privacy Breach Privacy Incidents can result in a Privacy Breach when the information compromised reveals any type of Personal Data. Privacy breaches may occur due to unauthorized access, unauthorized disclosure, misuse, theft or loss, etc.

Event/Breach Classification
Level Description
Critical The incident prevents continuity of one or more activities that are extremely critical to the organization’s operations, OR more than 20 users have been impacted.
High The incident prevents the use of equipment or of an application or system and this significantly affects the organization’s activities such incidents could be security policies violation, earthquakes, fire, storm ,IT system failure etc) OR more than 15 users have been impacted.
Medium The incident only affects one group of users and/or the disrupted activities are not critical to the organization’s operations.These include a major power or communication failure, network outage, etc) and some external man made and environmental incidents over wider area.OR more than 10 users have been impacted.
Low The incident only affects one person The disrupted activities are not critical to the organization’s operations.These include minor outages, network intrusions or failures and system crashes impacting few services/applications or limited to few business functions These are outages that will impact a part of a business or process that can be recovered in shorter times viz,.few minutes to less than an hour. OR less than 5 users have been impacted.
